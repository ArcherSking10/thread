Photos

Clear, well-lit, front-facing photos give the best results. We need to see the face clearly. Blurry, low-resolution, heavily filtered, or profile-only photos can limit how well we can capture likeness. If your photo isn't ideal, upload it anyway and we'll let you know in the preview stage.

No — we simplify or remove backgrounds by default. Our embroideries focus on the subject rather than a complex scene. We may include a subtle contextual element (a soft shape, a branch) if it adds to the composition, but we don't recreate full backgrounds.

We create photo-inspired keepsakes, not pixel-perfect reproductions. Embroidery is an artisan medium — it interprets, simplifies, and distills. We capture the spirit and recognizable likeness of the subject, not a photographic copy. We send you a preview sketch to approve before we stitch anything.

For now, please upload your best single photo. If you have additional reference images (different angles, a clear shot of a specific feature) you'd like us to use alongside it, include a note at checkout and email them to hello@silkthread.studio with your order number.

Production

  1. Order confirmed (within 1 hour by email)
  2. We create a simplified line-art preview and email it to you (within 48 hours)
  3. You approve it or request 1 small revision
  4. Our artisans begin stitching
  5. Piece ships to you (10–14 days total from order)

One round of small revisions is included — adjustments to names, minor positioning, or style details. Major changes (different photo, completely different composition) may require a new order. We send the preview specifically so you can catch anything before we start stitching.

Our pieces are crafted by skilled artisans trained in traditional Chinese embroidery techniques — including fine silk thread work, soft thread shading, and the layered needle methods that give our pieces their distinctive depth. All production is handmade; no machine embroidery.

Shipping

We quote 10–14 days from order to delivery for standard US orders. This includes production time (the majority) and domestic shipping (typically 2–5 days via USPS Ground Advantage). Expedited options available — email us before ordering.

Currently we ship within the United States only. International shipping is coming soon — sign up for our newsletter or email hello@silkthread.studio to be notified.

Free US shipping on orders over $80. Orders under $80 have a flat $8 shipping fee.

Returns & issues

Because each piece is custom-made to your order, we can't accept returns based on subjective preference (e.g. "it doesn't look enough like my pet"). This is exactly why we send a preview before stitching — please review it carefully. If your piece arrives damaged or with a clear production defect, contact us within 7 days and we'll make it right.

Email hello@silkthread.studio with your order number and photos of the damage within 7 days of delivery. We'll either remake the piece or issue a full refund, your choice.

Still have questions? Email us — we reply within 24 hours.
