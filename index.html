◈ FAQ ◈
Good to know

Before you order

Clear, front-facing photos with a simple background. JPG, PNG, HEIC accepted. We simplify backgrounds by default.
We create a photo-inspired embroidered keepsake — not a photographic replica. You'll receive a preview design for approval before stitching begins.
10–14 business days for production, plus 2–5 days US shipping via USPS Ground Advantage.
Made-to-order items cannot be returned for change of mind. Production defects are always made right.
Currently US domestic only. Contact us for international requests.
